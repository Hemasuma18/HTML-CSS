A Small intro on social Media:

Social media refers to digital platforms that allow users to create, share, and interact with content, fostering virtual connections and communities. Popular examples include Snapchat, Instagram,Youtube..etc, Initially designed for personal communication and networking, social media now plays a central role in marketing, entertainment, and information dissemination. Its usage spans across various demographics and has transformed how people communicate, consume news, and even form opinions. However, its widespread use has also raised concerns regarding privacy, mental health, misinformation, and digital addiction. Despite these challenges, social media remains a dominant force in modern society.
